About FABLAB


The Fab Lab at AIC-NITF is equipped with state of the art infrastructure containing equipments that range from basic mechanical tools to machineries with advanced technology suitable for designing and prototyping. Along with this, workshops are conducted on a monthly basis for students where training is imparted on varied topics to enhance their technical abilities.

Fab Lab Facilities:

  • 3D Designing & Printing
  • Laser Cutting
  • Laser Engraving
  • CNC Milling (small-scale)
  • PCB Milling (small-scale)
  • Vinyl Cutting
  • Robotics Training
  • Basic mechanical tools & devices
  • Other consumable IoT-based tools & devices
